What is the Moon Tea Podcast?

We're a podcast that focuses on craft, community, and building meaningful careers. We interview people like yourself and put it on the internet!

Where will the interview be published?

Youtube, Apple Podcasts, Spotify, and anywhere else you can listen to podcasts.

How long does the interview typically last?

Our interviews usually last around an hour, but we can adjust the time to fit the guest's schedule as needed.

Will I need to prepare anything beforehand?

We will send a brief questionnaire before the interview to cover relevant topics and avoid anything you're not interested in discussing. The questionnaire helps us be better hosts and helps us make your appearance on the MTP a big win for everyone involved.

What if I feel like I have nothing to talk about?

We understand that being interviewed can be nerve-wracking, especially if you're not used to it. Think of it as just having a cozy conversation that gets packaged nicely and put on the internet for our 13 to 14 listeners.

Can I promote my Etsy business, political candidacy, or favorite book during the interview?

Absolutely!

What if I change my mind and don't want the interview to be published?

No prob! Just let us know.
